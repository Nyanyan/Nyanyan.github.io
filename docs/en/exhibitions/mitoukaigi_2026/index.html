Mitou Kaigi 2026

Othello Professor Robot "Minoth" (2026)

It has been almost 30 years since an Othello AI defeated the world champion. In terms of strength, Othello AI is now almost perfect. However, what AI can do is still limited to just finding strong moves. What if there was a future where a strong AI had a robot body and could teach us how to win at Othello? We invite you to experience such a future. Why not try playing Othello with the robot's advice?

Details

Date: March 7, 2026 (Sat) 10:00-17:00

Venue: Tokyo Midtown Hall (9-7-2 Akasaka, Minato-ku, Tokyo 107-0052)

Admission: Free
