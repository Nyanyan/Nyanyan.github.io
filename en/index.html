Achievement

Mitou

What is Mitou?

"Mitou" is a project of IPA (Information-technology Promotion Agency, Japan) sponsored by the Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry. It implement and transform the world through the use of IT for the purpose of "finding and developing outstanding IT human resources." It discovers and nurtures such a genius creator in Japan.
Creaters under 25 years old promote their own projects in 9 months with about $20000 and advice from genius in Japan.
I was awarded for this project.
Learn more in Japanese

What did I (we) make?

We (coord.e and I) made MonoMotion in Mitou.
I mainly developped the hardware of robots and Reficere.

What is Super Creater?

At the end of each fiscal year, the “Mitou Super Creators” are selected. They are the creators whose project manager evaluated as being particularly excellent, and who reviewed by a third-party commitee and certified by IPA.
I was certified as a Super Creater. There are 327 Super Creaters in Japan as of 2018.
Learn more in Japanese

Achievement

Japan Physics Olympiad

What is Japan Physics Olympiad?

The Japan Physics Olympiad (JPhO) is a nationwide physics contest for young people under 20 years old and before enrolling in higher education institutions such as universities.
Learn more in Japanese

What is Silver Prize?

People who made excellent results in JPhO Final Challenge get Gold / Silver / Bronze / Excellent Prize
I got the Silver Prize (Top 18 people in Japan).

What is Most Excellent Experiment Prize?

In the first challenge, contestants write a report about experiments of a theme which is announced by the organizer.
People who wrote the most excellent reports get this prize.
I won the Most Excellent Experiment Prize (Top 4 in Japan).
